0.10.2 Release : A New Toy

The PiTiVi team is proud to announce the third release of the 0.10.x series of the PiTiVi opensource video editor.

Features of this release

  • UI improvements for usability
  • i18n support, translated in 12 languages
  • Rendering improvements
  • decodebin2 support
  • Critical bugfixes
